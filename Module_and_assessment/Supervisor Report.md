# Design Engineering Master's Project – Supervisor Report Sheet

This is the Supervisor Report Sheet for Design Engineering Master's Project.
It is for **Supervisors** to fill in about their assigned students.

The purpose is to contextualise and evidence aspects of the assessment criteria that only a supervisor can observe.
https://imperialcollegelondon.github.io/Design-Engineering-Masters-Project/Module_and_assessment/

Complete a submission for each student you **supervise**. You may save and return to your responses on submission.

## Questions
### Contextual Investigation
How did the student demonstrate independent thinking or original insight in defining the project within its broader context - especially in ways that extended beyond what was initially scoped or provided?

### Project Management
Can you describe how the student adapted their project plan or priorities in response to uncertainty, new risks, or changes in direction during the project?

### Design Engineering Process
What evidence did you observe of the student using a structured, iterative approach to develop their project - including how they selected and applied specific methods or tools?
Can you comment on the quality / correctness / appropriateness of their output or methodology?

### Validation
How did the student engage with validation and testing during the project - particularly in how they responded to results, refined their approach, or made decisions based on what they learned?'
